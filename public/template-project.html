Technology Preparation Training: Language courses for youth and women, 3 months

Target Start Date: October 29, 2011
Location: Nehum, Pakistan
$1580.00 still required

There is a high demand for computer training in the area, especially amongst the youth, and the digital phone line which the government has installed will be available for public use as of April. This will make it possible to use the internet locally.

A 3-month basic English course will be offered for 100 youth and women in the area to prepare them for computer and internet training and work.

Article Subhead

In addition, during the second hald of 2008 10 especially promising youth and females will be sent to a 6-month full-time English program in Islamabad which is subsidized and supported by the government.
